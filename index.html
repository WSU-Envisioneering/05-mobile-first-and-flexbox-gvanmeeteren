Overview

Tennis is a sport played either one-on-one (singles) or two-on-two (doubles), where players use a racket to hit a ball over a net into the opponentâ€™s side of the court. The goal is to hit the ball in a way that the opponent cannot return it within bounds.


Basic Structure

Court: Rectangular, divide by a net. boundaries differ slightly for singles and doubles.

Scoring: Points go 15, 30, 40 and game. You must win 6 games (by 2) to win a set, and most matches are best of 3 or 5 sets.

Serve: Players alternate serving each game, hitting from behind the baseline.
